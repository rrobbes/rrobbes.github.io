Romain Robbes

I am a "Directeur de Recherche" at CNRS since 2023. Earlier, I was an Associate Professor at the University of Chile, and the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano, Italy. Even earlier, I earned my Ph.D at the University of Lugano, Switzerland and studied at the University of Caen, France.

I work in the Progress Team (LaBRI, University of Bordeaux, France), where I conduct research at the interaction of Empirical Software Engineering and Machine Learning, with interests in Software Evolution, Mining Software Repositories, and Large Language Models.

I also have some responsibilities in the national and international research community.

You can find more information about my research and activities on DBLP, Google Scholar, and Researchr, where you can access my publications, service roles, and conference activities.
